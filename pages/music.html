"Outside Agitators"

"Outside Agitators" is the 2024 debut of local punk act Octopus Attacks Shark!!

Two sides of a degenerative coin, Outside Agitators takes a tonal adventure from forays into punk rock rhythmics and calls to action to a smoother avant-garde cleansing of the audial pallete.

Lyrics by Coyote
